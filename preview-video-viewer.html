About this video

BBC ‘Inside Out’ programme.

Broadcast, November 2012.

Sillitoe Trail is a digital reimagining of Alan Sillitoe’s novel ‘Saturday Night and Sunday Morning’ (1958). Commissioned by Arts Council England and the BBC as part of ‘The Space’, an experimental digital on-demand arts platform.

Produced by Paul Fillingham and James Walker with contributions from the Alan Sillitoe Memorial Committee, students, writers, artists and musicians, Sillitoe Trail represented a major content creation and digital workflow project for Thinkamigo.
