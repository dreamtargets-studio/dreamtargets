Award-winning Digital Arts Platforms

From our very first meeting at the Nottingham Writers Studio in 2010, writer and educator James Walker and I began working on digital platforms to celebrate the lives and work of our literary greats, engaging with readers in new ways.

Our digital storytelling platforms, made with the support of Arts Council England, the BBC, the Arts and Humanities Research Council, and leading Universities, have helped scores of creative practitioners find their voices.

Projects like the 'Sillitoe Trail' - a reimagining of the author's 1959 classic 'Saturday Night and Sunday Morning', or the zombie-genre digital comic 'Dawn of the Unread' - aimed at reluctant teenage readers, have blended the written word with video and live events in imaginative ways.

After winning The Guardian Award for teaching excellence in 2015, our creative case studies were a key element in the bid to secure UNESCO City of Literature status for Nottingham.
